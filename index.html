Bulk Agave Syrup & Inulin

The Tierra Group is a leading producer of premium agave based products, providing customers around the world with authentic organic agave syrup, inulin, Tequila, and agave-based spirits. With sales offices in the United States and Mexico we provide unsurpassed quality and service to our customers who are some of the largest food companies, distillers, and retailers in the world.

Any of our products can be ordered in various bulk amounts or in smaller retail sized packaging.

Where Our Blue Agave Comes From

The production of our agave products comes from over 50 years’ of experience in the planting, harvesting and industrialization of the blue agave tequilana weber. In 2007, our co-op of growers and entrepreneurs embarked on building the largest state of art facility in Mexico, producing the highest quality blue agave products.
